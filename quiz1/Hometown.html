Pekalongan

Here are some tourist attractions in Pekalongan Regency and City, Central Java, Indonesia:

City of Pekalongan:

1. Geographic Location: Pekalongan is situated on the northern coast of Java Island, making it an important port city in the region. The city is bordered by the Java Sea to the north.

2. Accessibility: Due to its location on the northern coast of Java, Pekalongan can be accessed by road, air, and sea transportation. There are highways and railways connecting this city to other cities in Central Java and its surrounding areas.

3. Port: Pekalongan has a significant port, which is used for trade and transportation of goods, especially the famous batik from this region.

4. Culture, Batik, and Santri Region: Pekalongan is renowned as the "Batik City" because it's one of the largest batik production centers in Indonesia. Batik is a traditional Indonesian art form that involves intricate fabric dyeing techniques. The city has various galleries and museums that introduce visitors to the art and culture of batik.
