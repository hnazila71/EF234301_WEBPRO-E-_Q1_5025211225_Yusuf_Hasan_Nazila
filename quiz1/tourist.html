Tourist Places

Image: Curug Bajing

Here are some tourist attractions in Pekalongan Regency and City, Central Java, Indonesia:

City of Pekalongan:

1. Batik Museum Pekalongan: This museum is a primary destination for batik enthusiasts. You can explore the history of Indonesian batik and view various beautiful batik collections.

2. Losari Beach: This beach also offers beautiful views and has a row of coconut trees that create a tropical atmosphere. It's a great place for relaxation and swimming.

Pekalongan Regency:

1. Curug Lawe: Curug Lawe is a hidden waterfall in Pekalongan Regency. To reach it, you'll need to do a light hike, but the natural scenery is well worth it.

2. Petungkriyono Village: There are two waterfalls, namely Curug Bajing and Curug Muncar, as well as Lake Mangunnan and Tugu Petungkriyono. This village offers an authentic rural tourism experience. You can witness the traditional coconut sugar-making process, learn about local farming, and experience the Javanese village atmosphere.
