Megono

Megono is a highly popular dish in the Pekalongan region and is often served at special occasions such as weddings or other significant events. Its savory and distinctive aroma makes it a beloved dish among many in the area, and it's also an integral part of Central Javanese culinary heritage.

Cassava Leaves: Cassava leaves are the main ingredient in Megono. These leaves are usually washed and boiled before being used in the dish.

Grated Coconut: Freshly grated coconut is also an essential component of Megono. It serves as a seasoning and imparts a rich, savory flavor to the dish.

Spices: Megono is typically cooked with traditional Javanese spices such as shallots, garlic, turmeric, galangal, ginger, red chili peppers, and salt. These spices are used to create the distinctive taste and delicious aroma.

Variations: There are variations in making Megono depending on the recipe and individual preferences. Some versions may include meat or fish as additional ingredients.
